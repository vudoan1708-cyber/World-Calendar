Instructions

Supported Countries for Searching

The Search Bar is used for searching different countries that are currently supported by the website. There are totally 230 available countries which can be looked up at one's own pace, by using ISO alpha-2 code.
(such as United Kingdom: UK, Korean: KR, Japan: JP, or Vietnam: VN,....).
For further information, please have a look at this website:
https://calendarific.com/supported-countries

Aims

One of The Main Purposes of this website is to provide general knowledge for everyone who takes interest in learning about different cultures, traditions via national holidays of every country in the world. The website is able to give the basic detail of all holidays of a chosen year from a specific country, as well as foresee holiday's date occurence a few years back or later

Guidances

The Welcome page appears with a board which is full of numbers ranging from 1 to 12 that represent the months of a year (this is called the Month Display section). Click/Press a number to see the month's holidays. You are now inside the Holiday Display section, then Click/Press on a holiday to see its description as well as the date it is celebrated on.
If you ever experience a page with no holidays on ALL THE MONTHS or a LOADING display for a long while when in the Holiday Display section, please make sure you are connected to an Internet, press F5 to refresh the page and try again
